Basic Theory Lesson

1. Introduction to Forex Trading

Forex trading involves exchanging one currency for another to profit from changes in currency values. It is the largest financial market in the world. Currency pairs like EUR/USD indicate how much of the quote currency is needed to buy one unit of the base currency.

2. Key Concepts

Bid and Ask Price: The bid is the price at which you can sell, and the ask is the price at which you can buy. The spread is the difference between them.

Leverage: Control a larger trade with a smaller amount of money.

Pips: The smallest price movement in a currency pair, typically 0.0001.

3. Technical Analysis

Forex traders use charts and patterns to analyze price movements, along with indicators like Moving Averages and RSI to identify trends.

4. Risk Management

Using stop-loss orders and proper position sizing helps manage risk and protects your account balance.

5. Psychology of Trading

Successful trading requires emotional control, avoiding decisions driven by fear or greed.

6. Conclusion

This lesson covers essential concepts in Forex trading. Continuous learning and practice are crucial for success.
